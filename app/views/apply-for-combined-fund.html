Energy bill support in Northern Ireland

This application is only for the £200 Alternative Fuel Payment in England, Scotland, and Wales.

Northern Ireland residents will receive a one-off automatic payment of £600 The payment is made up of £400 from the Energy Bills Support Scheme and the £200 Alternative Fuel Payment.

Some homes will not get the payment automatically. If you are not getting the payment automatically, you will be able to apply online soon.
